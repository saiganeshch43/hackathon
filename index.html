About Hackathon

A hackathon is a collaborative event where individuals or teams come together to engage in intensive, time-bound problem-solving. Participants, often from diverse backgrounds, work to create innovative solutions, typically in the fields of technology, software development, or design. Hackathons encourage creativity, teamwork, and rapid prototyping, fostering an environment for developing new and practical ideas or projects.
